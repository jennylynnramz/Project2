Looking at Evictions in San Francisco by Neighborhood

This group project took the dataset from Data SF to study the relationship between evictions and SF neighborhhods. Additionally, we took data from Redfin's website to look at the relationship, if any, in home prices.

Data sets used in this analysis:

  • San Francisco Evictions
  • San Francisco Real Estate Prices Scraped from Redfin

Explorations: The team wanted to dive into the evictions data to see if there were any geogrpahical patterns...some of the questions explored were:

  • Are there SF neighborhhods that have higher clusters of evictions?
  • What is the real estate "profile" of those neighborhhods?
  • What is the most common type of eviction?
  • Is there a relationship to the type of eviction with Neighborhood?
